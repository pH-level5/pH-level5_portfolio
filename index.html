ABOUT ME

Hey, I'm pH-level5, a Software Engineering student. Over time, that early curiosity grew into a genuine love for computer science and programming in general. Over the years, I've worked on numerous projects, big and small, though most were never meant for the public eye. I built this portfolio to curate and showcase those older projects that I feel are worth sharing, as well as to keep track of ideas and future projects that I may or may not pursue as I continue my studies.

CSS LIBRARY

Lunar Glass

A darkish, moody modern glassmorphism design library. This library provides a toned-down, simple, and reusable foundation of layered backdrop-filter glass, ambient scene radials, and SVG-noise grain textures. Lunar Glass CSS includes four built-in themes out of the box. Originally derived from an older platform project, the UI components and design system were curated into this lightweight, standalone CSS library.

CSS3 HTML5 Glassmorphism Themes

EXPERIMENTAL

Super Pong

A version of the almighty Pong game with a spin ;) This little minigame was a self-learning project. I wanted to create a small-scope browser game with a simple, playable design. Super Pong's main specialty is combining core Pong gameplay with extra elements, like the ability to spin the ball in two directions, different paddles, and unique balls that excel in certain playstyles.
