MindOS - Project Details

Phase 4 of 7 - In Active Development

What is MindOS?

MindOS is a personal management system built specifically for students and knowledge workers who deal with fragmented information across multiple platforms. It unifies notes, documents, task management, and daily training logs (Berichtsheft) into a single, cohesive workspace.

Think of it as a personal wiki + task manager + document organizer, all working together seamlessly. Your data stays local on your machine—no cloud required, no subscriptions, just a fast, privacy-focused productivity tool for your studies.

Core Features

  • Bidirectional Note Linking: Wiki-style `[[links]]` that connect your thoughts and create a knowledge graph
  • Task Management: Organize your assignments, study goals, and daily todos with priorities and due dates
  • Document Management: Hierarchical organization (Classes → Projects → Documents) with drag-and-drop
  • Interactive Graph View: Visualize connections between notes, tasks, and documents
  • Smart Search: Full-text fuzzy search across all content with tag filtering
  • Markdown Support: Rich markdown editor with live preview, YAML frontmatter, and Mermaid diagrams
  • Local-First: SQLite database, all data stays on your machine, works offline
  • Future: Optional AI Features: Bring your own API key or self-host AI for smart task suggestions and content analysis

Privacy & AI Features

Your Data, Your Control: MindOS stores all data locally on your machine using SQLite. No cloud sync required, no accounts needed.

Future AI Features (Optional): Planned AI features will require you to provide your own:

  • Self-hosted AI: Run models locally, data never leaves your machine
  • API Key (Cloud AI): Use services like OpenAI, Claude, etc. — data is sent to their servers (you control this)

Important: If you use a cloud AI service, your data will be sent to that provider according to their privacy policy. MindOS will clearly indicate when data leaves your machine. The choice is always yours.
